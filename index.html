  • Paracetamol

    • Paracetamol

    Paracetamol is a common pain reliever and fever reducer. It is often used to relieve mild to moderate pain from conditions such as muscle aches, toothaches, and headaches.

  • Aspirin

    • Aspirin

    Aspirin is a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used to treat pain, fever, and inflammation. It is also used to prevent blood clots and reduce the risk of stroke and heart attack.

  • Ibuprofen

    • Ibuprofen

    Ibuprofen is a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used to relieve pain, reduce inflammation, and lower fever. It is commonly used to treat conditions such as arthritis, menstrual pain, and minor injuries.
